How to Read This Chart

Think of the tech ecosystem as a seven-layer cake. The top layer is what end-users interact with (apps, SaaS). Each layer below supports the layers above. A company like Salesforce (top layer) depends on cloud infrastructure (Layer 6), which runs on physical servers & chips (Layer 7). Companies at higher layers are generally easier for AI to disrupt; lower layers have deeper moats. Performance badges show mkt-cap weighted stock returns for public companies ‚Äî data refreshes live. Click any layer to expand.
